# Hero Video Setup Guide

## Recommended Free Stock Videos

### From Pexels (Best Options):
1. **Sauna Videos**: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/sauna/
   - Look for: Steam rising, hot stones, water pouring
   - Example search: "sauna steam", "spa water", "wellness"

2. **Ice Bath/Cold Therapy**: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/ice%20bath/
   - Look for: Ice water, cold plunge, winter spa

### From Pixabay:
1. **Spa Wellness**: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/spa%20wellness/
2. **Sauna**: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/sauna/

### Specific Video Suggestions:
- Water being poured over hot sauna stones (creates steam effect)
- Mist/steam rising in a spa setting
- Calm water surface with ripples
- Ice crystals or cold water effects
- Person's silhouette in sauna (atmospheric)

## How to Add the Video:

1. Go to Pexels or Pixabay
2. Search for your preferred video
3. Download in 1080p (Full HD)
4. Save as `hero-video.mp4` in this folder
5. The website will automatically use it

## Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Duration: 10-30 seconds
- File size: Under 10MB
- No audio track needed

## Alternative: Direct Video URLs
Some stock video sites provide direct MP4 URLs. You can also use these by updating the video source in Hero.tsx.
