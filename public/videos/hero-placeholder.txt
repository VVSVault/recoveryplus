To add a wellness video background:

1. Download a free wellness/spa video from:
   - Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/wellness/
   - Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/search/wellness/
   
   Recommended searches: "spa", "wellness", "meditation", "yoga", "water", "nature"

2. Save the video as "hero-video.mp4" in this folder (/public/videos/)

3. Optimize the video:
   - Keep it under 10MB
   - 1920x1080 resolution
   - 10-30 seconds loop
   - No audio track

Example good videos from Pexels:
- Water ripples/waves
- Candles in spa setting  
- Person doing yoga/stretching
- Nature scenes (forest, beach)
- Steam/mist effects
